Harley-Davidson

The Harley-Davidson motorcycle has become an American icon. Harley-Davidson Motor Company is an American motor company. The brand name comes from the surnames of the company's founders, William Harley and Arthur Davidson.
It is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. It is the largest engine manufacturer in America and the world. Due to their speed, the company mostly produces for police stations. I and II. With the cooperation with the US army in the World Wars, it provided a large amount of income and reached the point it has reached today.

What's Harley-Davidson Vision?


Harley-Davidson defines motorcycle culture with its expanding range of pioneering, recognizable and customizable motorcycles, in addition to riding experiences and outstanding motorcycle accessories, riding accessories and apparel.
Harley-Davidson Financial Services provides financing, insurance and other programs to help Harley-Davidson drivers get on the road.

What's Harley Davidson Doing?

Harley-Davidson manufactures and sells premium quality custom, cruiser and touring motorcycles that offer cutting-edge technology, innovative design, distinctive sound and personalization possibilities. It also brings the brand to life with:


  • Driving experiences, training and events
  • motorcycle parts and accessories
  • Riding clothes and accessories
